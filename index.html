About DonBot

DonBot is a multipurpose bot designed to be the only bot your server needs, it is packed with features for everyday use.

Music To Your Ears

DonBot provides a wide range of music commands with many customization options, configurable DJ role and to give DJs full control of music.
DonBot can search YouTube and Soundcloud and can play Twitch livestreams, Bandcamp videos, hastebin playlists, direct links and more!
DonBot makes searching simple, just type in the title of the video and you'll get the search results!
You can easily repeat one song or the entire queue, shuffle it or reverse it with ease.

Great Tool For Moderation

DonBot is perfect for large servers that moderation is a huge part.
With DonBot's warning system, it's easy to keep track of members who constantly break the rules to punish them.
Alongside the warning system, DonBot provides commands to control the members, such as to:

  • Mute spammers for a duration of time
  • Softban members to delete their messages
  • Purge many messages at once

and more...
